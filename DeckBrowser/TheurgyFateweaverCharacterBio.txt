After months of crime-fighting in Rook City with her teammates in Extra-curricular, Theurgy has grown restless with her lack of knowledge in ley-line magic. imbuing the Scholar's Journal with magic brought forth an echo of his soul, but while helpful John has not had more knowledge to share than the contents of his journal.

In conversations with Lillian Corvus, Theurgy has learned some things about controlling power, and the general nature of various magickal styles, but insights into her particular flavor of magic must come from elsewhere. For this, Theurgy heads to Megalopolis seeking the counsel of Akash'Flora and the Argent Adept, and perhaps even her friend Windy from the Academy, in her journey to become...
