The Slaughterhouse Nine are a threat whispered about behind closed doors; a feral collection of parahuman serial killers with neither scruples nor mercy. They've operated for nearly fifteen years suffering setbacks and losses, but always managing to replace lost members with new parahumans, willing, not, or somewhere in between.

Jack Slash, their leader, is the sole remaining founding member. His power - extending the cutting edge of blades - isn't particularly strong, which leads people to suspect there might be something more to his long survival.

The Nine have recently suffered a loss, and now they're recruiting...
